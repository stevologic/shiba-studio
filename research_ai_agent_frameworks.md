# Latest AI Agent Frameworks Research (2024-2025)

## Top Frameworks
- **CrewAI**: Dominant for multi-agent team orchestration and structured workflows.
- **AutoGen** (Microsoft): Excellent for conversational multi-agent systems.
- **LangGraph**: Stateful agent graphs and complex controllable workflows.
- **OpenAI Swarm**: Lightweight multi-agent coordination.
- **LlamaIndex Workflows** & **Semantic Kernel**: Strong alternatives for RAG + agents.

## Trends from GitHub & Research
- Explosion of "agent skills" marketplaces and reusable skills.
- Focus on parallel agents, local-first tools, and coding agents.
- Emphasis on evaluation, memory, tool-use, and production reliability.
